Our Story

Preserving a Sacred Art Form

Sarvah Dance Academy was established in 2022 with the aim of teaching pure Bharatanatyam in the traditional Kalakshetra style. The academy focuses on building strong foundations in technique, discipline, expression, and stage presence while helping students grow with confidence and professionalism.

Over the past four years, Sarvah has proudly trained more than 100 students and continues to create a supportive environment that encourages passion, dedication, and artistic excellence.

Students have had the opportunity to perform on numerous prestigious stages across Ontario, gaining real-world stage experience that enriches their artistry and builds lasting confidence.

Our Mission

Authentic & Professional Training

Our mission is to provide authentic and professional Bharatanatyam training while preserving the richness and traditions of Indian classical dance. We strive to guide every student with personal attention, helping them develop technical skill, cultural understanding, confidence, and a lifelong love for the art form.

Our Vision

Inspiring the Next Generation

Our vision is to inspire and nurture the next generation of Bharatanatyam dancers who carry forward the beauty, discipline, and heritage of classical dance with grace and excellence. Sarvah Dance Academy aims to create confident performers and passionate artists who grow both on and off the stage.

Our Programs

Classes for Every Age

We welcome students of all ages with no age bar. Each batch is carefully structured to ensure progressive, meaningful growth in the art of Bharatanatyam.

🌸

Sub-Junior Batch

Kids · Foundation

Our youngest learners are introduced to the world of Bharatanatyam through playful, structured sessions designed to spark joy, curiosity, and a love for classical movement.

→ Ages 4 and above · 1 hr/week
🌺

Junior Batch

Kids · Developing

Building upon foundational skills, Junior students develop coordination, rhythm awareness, and expressive storytelling through traditional adavus and compositions.

→ Progressive placement · 1 hr/week
✨

Seniors Beginner

Adults · Beginner

An introduction to the fundamentals of Bharatanatyam for adult beginners. Learn basic posture, footwork, and hand gestures (mudras) in a warm, welcoming environment.

→ No prior experience needed · 1 hr/week
💫

Seniors Intermediate & Advanced

Adults · Intermediate + Advanced

Deeper exploration of adavus, complex compositions, abhinaya (expression), and performance refinement. Advanced students focus on choreography excellence and stage presence.

→ All levels welcome · 1 hr/week
Meet Your Guru

Smt. Gayatri Inaayat

Bharatanatyam Exponent · Choreographer · Educator

Smt. Gayatri Inaayat is a distinguished Bharatanatyam exponent with over 20 years of performance experience and more than a decade dedicated to teaching and choreography. Trained in the revered Kalakshetra tradition, she brings an unparalleled depth of classical knowledge to every class.

Through Sarvah Dance Academy, Gayatri continues to dedicate herself to preserving and propagating the Kalakshetra style of Bharatanatyam while mentoring the next generation of classical dancers with discipline, depth, and artistic excellence.

Achievements & Recognition

National Awardee of the prestigious Sangeet Natak Akademi, India's national academy of music, dance and drama

Bharatanatyam Scholarship recipient from the Centre for Cultural Resources and Training, under India's Ministry of Culture

Performed at the World Culture Festival, sharing stage with international artists from across the globe

Over 250 Bharatanatyam performances presented across India and Canada on prestigious stages

Conducted choreography workshops at Western University and Fanshawe College, London, Ontario
